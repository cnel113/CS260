Why Budget Stays?

Affordability, safety, and comfort for all travelers.

Budget Stays began when Phil and Erika Jackson booked a stay in Stockholm. The couple struggled to find accomodation that fit their budget. When they finally found something they were thrilled! They got there and quickly found questionable items left by the last guests, and the host was nowhere to be found.
This inspired the couple to create a business that helps people find pre-vetted, affordable places to stay. Now, you and your family can book accomodation without fear.

Our Values

Affordability

Enjoyable accomodation shouldn't be limited to the wealthy. Our locations fit any budget.

Safety

We interview accomodation owners and require deadlocks, outdoor lights, and more to keep you safe.

Comfort

We hold all hosts to high standards of cleanliness, basic ammenities, and adequate bedding to ensure your comfort
